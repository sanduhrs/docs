Troubleshooting

Please click the links below and your issue will be prefilled with our template. You need to replace the parts of the template where you see “{{some text}}”. Please check that someone else hasn’t already reported your issue in our issue tracker before reporting a new bug/enhancement/question.

  • Vulnerability: reports a security issue. These should be emailed to us! Don’t post anywhere else!
  • Bug: reports an feature that is not working as expected.
  • Enhancement: requests a removal, addition, or change of a feature.
  • Question: asks how a feature should be used or what the feature does.
